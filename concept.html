Space Sorting Business

Economic-strategy clicker
Audience: People who love strategy games in which you need to plan the distribution of their resources.
  • description Short description 2

    A space was discovered in space where people can live, but a lot of work needs to be done.

    A player is a businessman whom a business for the delivery of space resources wants to open. They are prepared at the warehouse. The player is committed to checking the quality of goods at the factory. The more boxes you check, the more money the player will receive.

    For this money, he can modernize production (speed up the process, hire more workers, put additional conveyors in the factory).

  • videogame_asset Game description 1
    Functions

    If a player misses a broken box, he loses the money invested for it. The player must feel that he is gaining new experience when his decisions help to accelerate the growth of his company, or see how to change his decisions if they bring losses. A player can be given a loan for a small amount (for example, $ 1000 if his income falls below 0). If the loan is over, then the game starts anew, or you can watch a one-time advertisement (the second time the advertisement is unavailable, and you have to start the game again. On the planet will be available 10 points of construction of factories. The goal of the game is to redeem all points before rivals (rivals - the program) if a point has already been bought, you will have to pay several times more for this place. Bonuses and boosts for advertising will be available in the game (the player may not use them and play without advertising) The uniqueness of the game is that in the economic system the player will need to learn how to make profitable decisions based on experience in the game. There will be random events in the game that will provide an opportunity to earn extra money, or lose a part of the income (for example, an employee in a factory gets sick, or a company won a grant)
